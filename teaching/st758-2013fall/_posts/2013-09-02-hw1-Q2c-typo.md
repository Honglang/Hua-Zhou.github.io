There is a typo in Q2\(c\) of homework 1. The correct binomial inversion formula should be  
$$
(A + UBV^T)^{-1} = A^{-1} - A^{-1} U (B^{-1} + V^TA^{-1}U)^{-1} V^T A^{-1}.
$$
